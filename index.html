Where I hope to be going, September 29, 2019

Currently, I am interested in data visualization tools and scientific computing. I hope that I will be able to assist in creating more efficient networks and workflows within many different fields of science. I have interests in people analytics within organizations and how to better measure individual productity and better categorize individual expertise. I'm deeply interested in transparent business structures and feel that software that can analyze people more precisely is a pre-requisite to making companies more transparent and effective.
